About Us

Our Story

Green Thumb Gardening was founded in 2010 by landscape architect Michael Green with a simple mission: to create beautiful, sustainable outdoor spaces that bring joy to our clients and harmony to the environment.

What started as a small operation with just two employees has grown into a full-service gardening company with a team of dedicated professionals serving the greater Portland area. Despite our growth, we remain committed to our core values of quality, sustainability, and exceptional customer service.

Over the years, we've had the privilege of transforming hundreds of outdoor spaces, from small residential gardens to large commercial landscapes. Each project is approached with the same level of care, creativity, and attention to detail.

We take pride in our work and are passionate about creating outdoor environments that not only look beautiful but also contribute positively to the ecosystem and provide functional spaces for our clients to enjoy.

2. Description of Services

Green Thumb Gardening provides professional landscaping and gardening services for residential and commercial properties. Our services include, but are not limited to, lawn maintenance, garden design, tree and shrub care, irrigation system installation and maintenance, and seasonal clean-up.

3. Scheduling and Appointments

All services are provided by appointment only. We require at least 48 hours' notice for rescheduling or cancellation. Failure to provide adequate notice may result in a cancellation fee of 25% of the scheduled service cost.

4. Payment Terms

Payment is due upon completion of service unless alternative arrangements have been made in advance. For ongoing maintenance services, monthly billing options are available. We accept cash, check, and all major credit cards.

For projects exceeding $1,000, a 50% deposit is required before work begins, with the remaining balance due upon completion.

5. Estimates and Pricing

All estimates provided are valid for 30 days and are based on our assessment at the time of the estimate. Additional work discovered during the project may require revised pricing. We will always consult with you before proceeding with any additional work not included in the original estimate.

6. Service Guarantee

We stand behind the quality of our work. If you are not satisfied with our services, please notify us within 7 days, and we will make reasonable efforts to address your concerns at no additional cost.

You are responsible for providing reasonable access to your property for our team to perform the agreed-upon services. This includes:

8. Weather Conditions
